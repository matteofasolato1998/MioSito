Who I Am?

Matteo Fasolato - Born in Thiene in 1998 I am a pianist, producer and teacher press Vicenza.
I began to study at the age of 10, starting with a classical path and then moving on to Blues music, Stride.
I have Graduated at the Pollini Conservatory of Padua at the address of Piano Jazz.

I studied digital composition and audience chill, lo fi, electronic music and soundtracks on my social channels.
Currently I study music production in ableton live and I follow various mix & master courses.
