Matteo Fasolato

Pianista, Produttore, Compositore

My name is Matteo Fasolato, I am 24 years old and I am a Pianist, Producer from Thiene, Vicenza.

.

I began my studies of music at 10 years old with a private teacher and later at a music institute in Thiene. At the age of 16, I obtained pre-academic classical piano certification and theory and solfege certification.

From the age of 16 I moved my interest towards a niche genre,Ragtime, making me an expert after several years of study. The artists I played were Scott Joplin, Jelly Roll Morton, James P. Jhonson, Eubie Blake ecc.

After studying ragtime I approached the Blues, Jazz and so I attended the conservatory in Pianofort Jazz at the Conservatorio Pollini in Padua, of which I obtained a Degree in 2021 at 22 years.

During the studies of the conservatory I was interested in the world of electronic/digital music, then studying the fundamental concepts of sound and becoming familiar with the various Daw and plugins on the market.

I started with Fl studio 12 with simple Trap/Rap beats up to Huose, Lo-Fi or Orchestral tracks

In 2020 I did a 60-hour course about Using Ableton Live 10, Producing a song, mixing and sound theory.

To date I have been producing for 5-6 years all kinds of music and in recent years I have chosen Ableton as Daw.

I prefer the compositions of Orchestral Pieces or soundtracks and I continue to study for possible collaborations.
